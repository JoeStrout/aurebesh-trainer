This app teaches you how to read the Aurebesh (Star Wars) font, used extensively at the Galaxy's Edge areas of the Disney parks, as well as in many Star Wars games, movies, and other media.

How To Learn Aurebesh

Tap the circular ? (?) button in the top-right corner to see a reference card for the Aurebesh alphabet.

Try to read the word shown, then tap the big blue CHECK (Check) button. If you got it right without help, tap the green GOOD (Good) button; otherwise tap the red MISSED (Missed) button.

For more information, go to: https://github.com/joestrout/aurebesh-trainer
